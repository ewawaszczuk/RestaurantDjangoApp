Our Story

The Fresco Basilico story begins in Poland, Opole in the 1960â€™s. Janina Nowak found a recipe in the old desk at her attic. It was a recipe for homemade pizza. She have never cook something like that before but she decided to try. It appears that everyone loved her pizza and that would inspire her to open a small Italian Restaurant, but she eventually gave up her dream of owning her own business in order to raise her small children, Patric and Jack.

In 1980 with their children grown, Janina and her family decided to pack up all of their belongings and move to Warszawa to make one last attempt at owning their own business. With the last of their savings, they opened Fresco Basilico Restaurant and began selling pizzas. Through hard work and determination their business grew to a modest size. With great foresight and intuition, their son Patric decided to help his parents and add some italian pastas to the menu. It was a hit! Somehow he just knew that guests would enjoy a restaurant with all kinds of italian meals. The restaurant was an immediate success and it is still so today.
