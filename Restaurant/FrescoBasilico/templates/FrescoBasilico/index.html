We are Fresco Basilico

Restaurant Fresco Basilico since 1960 gives you the opportunity to taste Italian cuisine at its best - this is confirmed by our regular guests, among whom there is no shortage of native Italians!!.

With us, everyone can feel like in Italy. Whether you're in a suit and tie or in a hoodie, you'll be well looked after. Every day we work on the taste and quality of the dishes served, we prepare all the dishes directly to the guests' request.

We use the highest quality original and fresh Italian products that can not be replaced. However, fruit, vegetables, herbs, fresh meat, fish and eggs are obtained by small local producers who, just like us, are aware of how important healthy food is.
